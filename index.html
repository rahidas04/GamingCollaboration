Gaming Together

Collaboration and Community in Modern Video Games

Overview

Video games are often seen as isolating, but many modern titles are built on collaboration and community. From fast-paced tactical shooters to gentle farming simulators and live-streaming culture, games bring people together in unique and meaningful ways.

This site explores four types of games: Multiplayer Competitive, Open-World Roleplaying, Narrative Games, and Stream Culture. This website will show how they each create new forms of social connection.
